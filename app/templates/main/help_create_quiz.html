Creating a Quiz

AI-Quizzer offers two types of quizzes: Question-based and Language Conversation. Here's how to create each:

Question-based Quiz

  1. Select "Questions" as the quiz type.
  2. Upload images:
    • Select images from your device or upload from your phone.
    • You can use pictures of book pages or any study material.
  3. AI Generation:
    • Gemini Vision AI automatically generates relevant questions based on your uploaded images.
    • Questions are grounded in your images, ensuring accuracy and relevance.
  4. Review and Edit:
    • Review the generated questions on the screen.
    • Edit or add questions if needed.

Language Conversation Quiz

  1. Select "Language" as the quiz type.
  2. Set up the quiz:
    • Add conversation prompts for various scenarios or topics.
    • Specify the target language for practice.
  3. AI Integration:
    • Gemini multimodal AI powers the conversational feature.
    • It will evaluate user responses based on the prompts and language settings.

With AI-Quizzer, you can create personalized quizzes in minutes. It's free and easy to use at ai-quizzer.com!
